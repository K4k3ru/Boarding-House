Comfortable sleeping quarters with quality mattress, storage space, and peaceful atmosphere for rest. Each bedroom accommodates 2-4 people with adequate lighting for a comfortable stay.

Basic kitchen area with 1 sink and 1 faucet for meal preparation and cleaning. The kitchen provides essential facilities for washing dishes and preparing simple meals in a clean, functional space.
